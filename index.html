Built for Focus & Flow.

Distractions are the enemy of deep work. NUMA helps you stay in the zone with integrated to-do lists, a minimalist interface, and a focus mode that clears the clutter.

It's your command center for getting things done.

Modular & Customizable

Why settle for generic? NUMA lets you shape your homepage to match your vibe—whether you're working late at night or planning your day in the morning. Toggle between light and dark themes to suit your mood, apply a monochrome filter for a cleaner aesthetic, or hide elements like the clock, greeting, and quotes to remove distractions.

Every option is just a switch away, giving you quiet control over your space.

Open Source

NUMA is fully open source—every line of code is transparent and available on GitHub. You can audit it, modify it, or contribute to its future. No accounts, no tracking, no hidden scripts. Everything runs locally in your browser, ensuring complete privacy and offline access.

NUMA is a community-driven tool, built for users, by users.
